What is the difference between PLA and Resin prints?

When choosing between PLA and Resin prints, the most important thing to know is that Resin holds more details but is more brittle, versus PLA which is stronger but doesn't hold details as well.

What alloy of aluminum is used?

EOS' aluminum family of powders encompasses the AlSi10Mg, AlF357 and Al2139 AM alloys which are typical aluminum-silicon casting alloys.

What is your pricing model?

We use a made-for-order pricing method. Each order is unique, so each price is unique. The bigger the order the cheaper the ml of material is.

What is the price difference between Resin and PLA

The price difference between Resin and PLA is approximately 50% more for Resin.

How long do prints take to finish?

Obviously there are a lot of parameters to that question. But if we take a 10cm model of a human and print it the results are as follows: Resin - 8.5 hrs, PLA - 6.2 hrs and Aluminum - 10.5 hrs.

How precise are the aluminum printers?

They can print a minimum height layer of 100 micrometers. So they are not exactly made for precision and art like PLA or Resin printers, they are intended to be used for machine parts or utensils.
